findInverse :: Integral a => a -> a -> a -> a Source #

For a, p, q, finds the multiplicative inverse of a in \(\mathbb{Z}/pq\mathbb{Z}\). That is, finds e such that \[ ae \equiv 1 \pmod{\varphi(pq)} \].
